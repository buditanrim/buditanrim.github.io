Hi. I'm Budi Tanrim.

A Head of Design, a framework fanatic, a collaborative complex problem solver, a business-aware designer.

I'm currently leading design team of 90+ people at Bukalapak. I'm in the mission to embed and sustain the human-centered practice in the company, creating safe and constructive design cultures, and empower team through framework and methods.

Previously, I worked with Shopify to improve the onboarding experience and defining the icon language and principles. Before that, I worked with various companies during my time as a freelancer: Yahoo, Wealthsimple, Marvelapp, Zerply, and more!

I created icon foundry called Budicon in 2013. It used and helped a lot of companies like inVision, Hotjar, Adobe, Creative Market, and Disney.

Co-creator. I believe in collaborative process to gain multidisciplinary perspective to solve wicked problems

Framework fanatic. I develop and apply frameworks for my team to make impact at scale

Business aware. I connect the dots to produce viable, valuable, feasible product to achieve desired outcome.
