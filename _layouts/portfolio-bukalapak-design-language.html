Bukalapak Design Language

When I joined Bukalapak, our design language was disjointed. The desktop was inconsistent with the apps, we had 6 style of icons, the email was outdated and confusing. At a large scale company with 40+ products, how might we increase design cohesiveness?

Bukalapak for Web

Many buyers still prefer browsing through desktop. However, our desktop web design language was outdated.

The problem landscape

To better understand the problem, I initiated a co-creation session with everyone from each function — marketing, legal, product, business, engineering, design, and data — to share their pain points during the discovery and development phases. There are three main root issues that we wanted to explore: collaboration, idea validation during discovery, and vision.
