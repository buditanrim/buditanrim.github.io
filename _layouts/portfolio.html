Humanizing bookkeeping for micro business in emerging market

Context - Meet Yuli

If you ever go to Indonesia, you will likely find this humble shop like the one owned by Yuli. People who live around go here to buy daily needs, like rice, snacks, or shampoo. The thing is, Yuli has to record all the transactions on the paper and manually recap to know whether her business is on profit or loss. Yuli is one of the millions of other micro businesses we empower. Our success is their success.

As a design lead, I support the multidisciplinary team to navigate from the ambiguous state, facilitating the design activities to quickly achieve alignment, and continuously validating our ideas with real customers to achieve product-market fit.

The challenge

Empower micro business in the emerging market to be more aware of their business financial.

The outcome

A simple experience to record their financial, monitor their business performance, and make informed decisions.
